1. Upload foppro.zip to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Create a new project and get his [Script ID](https://www.useloom.com/share/edf26848f8c14c8a969bbc3dc09acb3d).
1. Enter the script Id on the settings of foppro plugin and save it

= How does FOP Pro know my customer’s name? =

FOP Pro is passed information from the autoresponder (if your customer arrived through an e-mail link) or from Facebook (if they didn’t).
So when you use the [NAME] shortcode in your FOP Pro message, it’ll just be replaced witht he actual name of your prospect.
